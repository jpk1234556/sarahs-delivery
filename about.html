About Sarah's Delivery

Learn more about our company and commitment to excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Sarah Ingabire, Sarah's Delivery began as a small local courier service with a vision to provide exceptional delivery services with a personal touch. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted name in the logistics industry, serving thousands of businesses and individuals across the nation.

Our Mission

At Sarah's Delivery, our mission is to connect people and businesses through reliable, efficient, and professional courier services. We believe that every package tells a story, and we're committed to ensuring that story has a happy ending.

Why Choose Us

  • Experience: Over 15 years in the delivery industry
  • Reliability: On-time delivery guarantee
  • Professionalism: Uniformed staff with extensive training
  • Technology: Advanced tracking and communication systems
  • Customer Service: Dedicated support team available 24/7
